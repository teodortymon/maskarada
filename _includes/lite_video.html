{% comment %} Lazy-loading YouTube facade via lite-youtube-embed (vendored in t/js/lite-youtube). Loads only a thumbnail until the user clicks, then swaps in the real player — keeps pages fast when many videos are on screen (card grid, hidden modals). Params: video - full YouTube embed URL from _s2 (https://www.youtube.com/embed/VIDEOID) (required) title - accessible play title for the play label (optional) params - extra iframe query params, e.g. "color=white&playsinline=1&rel=0" (optional) The video id is the last path segment after /embed/, with any ?query stripped. {% endcomment %} {% assign vid = include.video | split: '/embed/' | last | split: '?' | first %}
