Design Option 3: Hero Section Banner (Homepage Only)

Pros:

  • Creates a stunning first impression on the homepage
  • Logo appears with animation for extra impact
  • Navigation stays compact and sticky for easy access
  • Includes tagline/subtitle for context
  • Theatrical gradient background enhances the curtain effect
  • Only on homepage - other pages remain clean

Cons:

  • Only visible on the homepage (not on other pages)
  • Requires scrolling to see content below

Best for: Creating a memorable welcome experience while keeping navigation clean and functional

This design treats the logo as a hero element that welcomes visitors to your homepage. The animation and theatrical presentation create excitement and anticipation, perfect for a children's theatre. The sticky navigation ensures users can always navigate easily.
