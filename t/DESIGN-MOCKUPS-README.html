My Recommendation

Option 3 (Hero Section) offers the best balance:

  • Creates maximum impact on the homepage where it matters most
  • Keeps other pages clean and content-focused
  • Sticky navigation ensures accessibility
  • Works beautifully on mobile devices
  • Includes space for your tagline/subtitle

Runner-up: Option 4 (Compact Sticky) if you want the logo visible on every page throughout the browsing experience.
